Mathematics Tools

These tools are currently available. None of them satisfy me.

Isabelle Too limiting
Coq Too limiting
HOL Rebuilds everything
Phox Too arcane
Mizar Too much notation

These are just some of the reasons for rejecting these tools. I do not need tools that are bloated with features, too complex, or too limiting from the perspective of mathematics. Some tools, such as HOL, rebuild all theorems everytime they are loaded, which means that all effort gets reduplicated. Instead, the tool I want should build on previously built materials without reduplication.

The QED Project

The QED Project is very promising, but apparently has never gotten underway. The idea is to form a community around the automation of mathematics.
